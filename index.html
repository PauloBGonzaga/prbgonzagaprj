Presenting 4 programs:

1. Conversion - Capable of Fahrenheit to Celsius, Celsius to Fahrenheit, Meters to Feet, and Feet to Meters.

2. Income Tax Calculator - Provides income tax based on provided income and tax bracket.

3. Looping - Contains 3 Loop programs, first is a Factorial (while) loop, second is a Sum (do while) loop, and finally third an Average (for loop).

4. Simple Payroll - Capable of storing data for each employee inputted, displays table of data, and finally allows for individual deletion of employee data as shown in the table.
